A Beginner's Journey to Ceramics

Ceramics. It may seem to be just a hobby - but it's more. It's an art, a skill, it's a way of life. Venturing on this journey is one that takes stamina, fortitude, and dertermination. But these sacrifices are worth it, for every morsel of hard work you put into honing the craft, you will find tenfold the amount of satisfaction. With that etablished, let us delve into my molding moments, my clayful beginnings, a glimpse into a ceramic beginner's diary. I hope you are as fired up to hear about my kilnside reflections as I am to convey these earthen explorations to you.

The First Step

A journey of a thousand miles begins with one step, and the first step to beginning one's relationship with ceramics begins with the careful curation of a pinch pot. The ideal position for a novice to begin, this pinch pot allows the aspiring ceramicist to get a feel for the clay.
